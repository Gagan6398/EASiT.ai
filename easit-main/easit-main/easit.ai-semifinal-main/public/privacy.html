Section 1

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture provided through Google Sign-In authentication.
  • User Queries: Text and voice inputs you submit to the AI assistant.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through our third-party payment processor (we do not store full credit card numbers).
  • Communications: Any messages or feedback you send to our support channels.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type and version, operating system, device type, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, interaction timestamps, and feature preferences (e.g., smart preset selections).
  • Network Information: IP address, approximate geographic location (city/country level), and referring URLs.
  • Performance Data: Page load times, error logs, and service performance metrics.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: Profile information (name, email, profile picture) authorized by you during authentication.
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregated usage and demographic data.
Section 2

AI Data Processing

How your data flows through our AI: When you submit a query, it is sent to Google's Gemini AI for processing with real-time web search grounding. This section explains how AI-specific data handling works.

2.1 Query Processing

When you interact with Easit.ai:

  • Your text or voice input is transmitted to Google's Gemini AI API for response generation.
  • Queries may be sent to Google's web search services for real-time grounding and verification.
  • Hallucination-checking processes may analyze outputs before delivery to you.

2.2 Voice Data

When using voice interaction features:

  • Audio is captured by your browser and converted to text using speech recognition.
  • We process the transcribed text, not raw audio recordings.
  • Voice data is not stored after the transcription process is complete.

2.3 AI Model Training

We do not use your personal queries or conversations to train or fine-tune AI models. Your interactions are used solely to provide the Service to you and for aggregate analytics purposes.

2.4 Data Shared with AI Providers

Search context Google Search Web grounding Per Google's terms
Smart preset config Not shared Local feature Session-based

Section 10

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

🔍 Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✏️ Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

🗑️ Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

⏸️ Right to Restrict

Request restricted processing of your data in certain cases.

📦 Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🚫 Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.

🤖 Automated Decisions

Right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing.

↩️ Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@easit.ai. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or within the timeframe required by applicable law). We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to delete personal information held by businesses.
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal data.
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.
