# Privacy Policy

**Easit.ai Inc.**
**Effective Date: July 1, 2026**
**Last Updated: July 1, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Easit.ai Inc. ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our AI assistant platform ("the Service").

This policy complies with applicable data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA).

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and profile picture (obtained via Google Sign-In OAuth)
- **Chat Content**: Messages, queries, and prompts you submit to the AI assistant
- **Voice Data**: Audio input during voice interactions (processed in real-time, NOT stored as audio files)
- **Persona Settings**: Your tone, style, and verbosity preferences
- **Payment Information**: Billing details processed through third-party payment processors (we do not store card numbers)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Interaction patterns, feature usage, session duration, query frequency
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, pages viewed, error logs
- **Performance Metrics**: Response times, confidence scores, search grounding frequency

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Google Sign-In**: Basic profile information authorized by your Google account
- **Google Gemini API**: AI model responses (processed through Google's infrastructure)
- **Google Search**: Search results used for real-time grounding (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:
- **Service Delivery**: Processing your queries, generating AI responses, maintaining conversation history
- **Personalization**: Applying your persona settings (tone, style, verbosity) to AI interactions
- **Verification**: Performing hallucination checks and search grounding to ensure response accuracy
- **Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the Service (using anonymized, aggregated data only)
- **Support**: Responding to your inquiries and providing technical assistance
- **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- **Communication**: Sending service updates, security alerts, and (with consent) marketing communications

## 4. How We Store Your Data

### 4.1 Chat History
- **Authenticated Users**: Chat history is stored securely and synced across your devices
- **Guest Users**: Chat history is stored locally in your browser's localStorage and is not transmitted to our servers
- **Retention**: Chat history is retained for as long as your account is active, or until you manually delete it

### 4.2 Voice Data
- Voice interactions are processed **entirely in real-time memory** via secure WebSocket connections
- Raw audio is **NEVER stored** on our servers
- Voice data is discarded immediately after processing

### 4.3 Encryption
- All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
- All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
- API keys and sensitive credentials are stored using environment-level encryption

## 5. How We Share Your Information

We do **NOT** sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

### 5.1 Service Providers
- **Google Cloud**: AI model hosting and search grounding (subject to Google's Data Processing Agreement)
- **Payment Processors**: For processing subscription payments (they access only payment-specific data)
- **Hosting Providers**: For infrastructure and content delivery

### 5.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

### 5.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction. You will be notified of any such transfer.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 For All Users
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your conversation history at any time
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

### 6.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- Right to data portability
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent
- Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### 6.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

### 6.4 DPDPA Rights (Indian Users)
- Right to access and correct personal data
- Right to erasure of personal data
- Right to nominate a representative
- Right to grievance redressal

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Access controls and authentication for internal systems
- Incident response procedures for data breaches

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (for EU data transfers)
- Compliance with applicable local data protection laws
