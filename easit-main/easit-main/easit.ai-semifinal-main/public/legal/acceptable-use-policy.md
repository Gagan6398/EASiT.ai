# Acceptable Use Policy

**Easit.ai Inc.**
**Effective Date: July 1, 2026**

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") defines the boundaries of acceptable behavior when using the Easit.ai platform. Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination.

## 2. Prohibited Content

You may NOT use Easit.ai to generate, request, or distribute:

### 2.1 Harmful Content
- Content promoting violence, terrorism, or self-harm
- Instructions for creating weapons, explosives, or dangerous substances
- Content sexualizing minors in any form
- Non-consensual intimate imagery or deepfakes

### 2.2 Illegal Content
- Content that violates any applicable law or regulation
- Phishing, social engineering, or fraud-related content
- Content facilitating drug trafficking or other illegal trade
- Copyright-infringing material (knowingly)

### 2.3 Abusive Content
- Harassment, bullying, or threats directed at individuals or groups
- Hate speech targeting race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected classes
- Defamatory or libelous statements

### 2.4 Deceptive Content
- Disinformation or deliberately misleading content intended to deceive
- Impersonation of real individuals or organizations
- Content designed to manipulate public opinion through fake narratives

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 System Abuse
- Attempting to bypass safety filters, content policies, or identity protections
- Prompt injection attacks or jailbreaking attempts
- Reverse-engineering, decompiling, or extracting model weights
- Automated scraping, crawling, or systematic data extraction

### 3.2 Rate Limits and Fair Use

| Tier | Verified Searches/Day | Voice Minutes/Day | Messages/Hour |
|---|---|---|---|
| Free | 25 | 60 | 100 |
| Pro | Unlimited | Unlimited | 500 |

Exceeding these limits may result in temporary throttling. Persistent abuse may result in account suspension.

### 3.3 Commercial Restrictions
- Do not resell or redistribute API access without written permission
- Do not use the Service to build competing AI assistant products
- Do not use the Service for bulk content generation without a commercial license

## 4. Enforcement

### 4.1 Violation Response
| Severity | Action | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | Warning + content removal | Borderline inappropriate language |
| Moderate | 24-hour suspension | Repeated attempts to bypass filters |
| Severe | Permanent ban | Illegal content, harassment, CSAM |
| Critical | Ban + law enforcement referral | Terrorism, child exploitation |

### 4.2 Appeals
If you believe your account was suspended in error, you may appeal by emailing support@easit.ai with your account details and a description of the situation. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.

## 5. Reporting

To report a violation of this AUP, contact us at:
- Email: abuse@easit.ai
- In-app: Use the "Report" button on any message

All reports are reviewed within 48 hours.

## 6. Changes

We may update this AUP at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email or in-app notification.
