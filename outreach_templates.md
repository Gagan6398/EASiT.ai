## Quick Reference: Personalization Hooks by Investor Type

| Investor Type | Personalization Angle |
|---|---|
| **AI-Native Funds** | Reference their AI thesis; mention technical architecture (Gemini, grounding engine) |
| **Tier-1 VCs** | Lead with market size and traction metrics; emphasize defensibility |
| **Enterprise AI** | Focus on enterprise API roadmap; mention compliance and reliability |
| **India/Global Seed** | Highlight global + India market opportunity; cost-efficient team |
| **Accelerators** | Emphasize coachability, speed of iteration, and team |
| **Angel Investors** | Keep it personal; reference their content/tweets; be concise |

## Email Best Practices Checklist

- [ ] Subject line is under 50 characters
- [ ] Email body is under 200 words (cold outreach)
- [ ] Personalized opening line references investor's specific work
- [ ] One clear CTA (call, meeting, or reply)
- [ ] No attachments in first email (use links instead)
- [ ] Sent Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM recipient's timezone
- [ ] BCC your CRM / tracker email for logging
- [ ] Proofread investor name, fund name, and pronouns
